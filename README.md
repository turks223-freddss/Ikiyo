# Ikiyo
Many people struggle with maintaining motivation to stick to routines and complete tasks despite their initial determination. While lifestyle tracking apps can help with goal-setting and organization, they often rely on self-reporting, which lacks real-time accountability. Research suggests that social support and engagement features improve motivation and task completion. A proposed solution is a lifestyle tracker app that includes partner verification instead of self-reporting and incorporates gamification elements to enhance motivation, accountability, and user retention.
